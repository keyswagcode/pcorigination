# KREC — Underwriting Guidelines Volume II

Long-Term Investment Lending (DSCR 1-4 Unit, DSCR 5-8 Unit / 2-8 Mixed-Use, DSCR Foreign National, Non-QM Owner-Occupied)

Effective: April 2026 · Version 1.0

> KREC's long-term investment + consumer-purpose products. Chapter 14 Salability Matrix references investor codes V (flagship DSCR+NonQM), F (parallel DSCR+NonQM+ITIN+2nd lien), L (DSCR only, Bridge Choice), C (DSCR business-purpose only, ineligible ND/NV/SD/UT), K (Volume I MF Bridge crossover only).

---

## Product summary

| Family | Variants | Occupancy | Purpose |
|---|---|---|---|
| DSCR Rental | Standard (1-4), Expanded (1-4), STR, 5-8 / 2-8 Mixed-Use, Foreign National | NOO only | Business |
| Non-QM Owner-Occupied | Full Doc, Bank Statement, P&L, 1099, Asset Utilization, Super Jumbo, ITIN, Foreign National Owner-Occ, Closed-End Second Lien | Primary / 2nd Home (some investment) | Consumer (mostly) |

## Geography

**Fully ineligible (all Vol II):** ND, SD, VT.
**DSCR ineligible by specific market:** Washington DC investment, Cook County IL (most investors), Baltimore City MD, Indianapolis IN, Lubbock TX, Patterson NJ, FL Charlotte/Lee/Hendry/Glades Counties, Hawaii Lava Zones 1-2.
**Memphis TN:** wholesale field review required, transfer appraisals not accepted.
**NV/UT:** ineligible on Investor C.
**Non-QM owner-occ:** HI Non-QM limited to delegated correspondents; IL Cook/Kane/Peoria/Will County TRID ineligible (except correspondents); MS/NJ/TX ineligible for 2nd liens; NY wholesale TRID halted by one major investor eff July 2025.
**STR ineligible:** NYC 5 boroughs; Philly row homes; DSCR Multi; DSCR Foreign National.
**Texas SB 17:** Non-permanent resident aliens from China/Russia/Iran/NK — primary residence only (investment/2nd home ineligible).
**Texas 50(a)(6):** home equity on homestead; 12-day waiting period, 3-day rescission, 80% CLTV cap, 2% fee cap.

## Borrower structures

**DSCR eligible entities:** LLC, LP, LLP, C-Corp, S-Corp, inter vivos revocable trusts (with Certificate of Trust).
**DSCR individual vesting permitted** on most investors (NOT permitted in CA/NJ/IL on some).
**Non-QM owner-occ:** individual or revocable trust only; entity vesting not allowed.
**Foreign National DSCR:** entity-only vesting required; max 2 FNs as guarantors; US registered agent; US depository assets; valid passport + non-tourist visa.
**Ineligible:** irrevocable trusts (except inter vivos revocable), land trusts, community land trusts, co-ops, fund LLCs, foreign entities, non-profits.

## Liquidity & reserves

**DSCR PITIA reserves scale with loan amount:**
- ≤ $1M: 3-6 mo
- $1-1.5M: 6 mo
- $1.5-2M: 6-9 mo
- $2-2.5M: 9-12 mo
- > $2.5M: 12-18 mo
- DSCR Multi: 6 mo min
- DSCR Foreign National: 6-12 mo
- DSCR First-Time Investor: 12 mo
- DSCR No-Ratio: 12 mo
- DSCR STR: 6 mo
- Cash-out proceeds may satisfy reserves (except DSCR Multi + Foreign National on some investors)

**Non-QM PITIA reserves:**
- ≤ $1M: 2-6 mo
- $1-2M: 6-9 mo
- > $2M: 12 mo
- Super Jumbo: 12-24 mo
- ITIN: 6 mo min

**Exposure caps:**
- Standard DSCR + Non-QM: $10M / 10 loans aggregate
- Cash-out transactions: $5M / 5 loans
- DSCR Multi: $5M / 5 loans
- DSCR No-Ratio: $7.5M / 10 loans aggregate; $5M / 5 cash-out
- Non-QM Super Jumbo + Foreign National: case-by-case Committee

**Ineligible assets:** pledged, non-borrower assets, fixed/illiquid, unsecured loan proceeds, custodial POD/TOD/ITF accounts, unliquidated crypto, income-producing assets already in income calc.

## Property eligibility — DSCR 1-4 Unit

**Eligible:** NOO SFR, 2-4 unit, townhome, PUD, warrantable condo, non-warrantable condo (Expanded -5% LTV, 1.00+ DSCR, 700+ FICO), condotel (Expanded max 70% LTV purchase/65% cash-out), site condos.

**DSCR 5-8 / 2-8 Mixed-Use:** 5-8 unit residential; 2-8 mixed-use where residential sqft ≥ 50% AND commercial income ≤ 49.99%. Commercial uses limited to retail/office/professional (no heavy industrial/automotive). Max 1 vacant unit (2-3), 2 (4-6), 3 (7+). No vacant commercial space.

**Universal ineligible:** owner-occupied on DSCR, co-ops, mobile/manufactured not permanent, commercial, unique/specialty/luxury, agricultural/working farms, assisted living, Hawaii Lava Zones 1-2, 4+ unit condos >25yrs old in FL, active solar leases without reduction, deed-restricted, Section 8 (Standard; permitted Expanded), legal non-conforming that can't rebuild (Standard).

**Rural:** generally ineligible on DSCR Standard; Expanded may permit with -5% LTV.

**STR:** C3+ condition, furnished, 10% extraordinary expense haircut, AirDNA/12-mo history/appraiser STR addendum, third-party STR legality confirmation (Property Guard, Vrolio).

**PadSplit:** refinance only, 70% LTV, 1.15 DSCR, 1-yr PPP min. Non-PadSplit SRO ineligible.

**Florida sinkhole counties:** Pasco, Hernando, Hillsborough, Pinellas, Citrus, Marion, Lake, Polk, Sumter, Orange, Charlotte, Sarasota, Collier.

## DSCR calculation

**Formula:** `DSCR = Qualifying Monthly Rent ÷ Monthly Debt Service (PITIA or ITIA)`

**Minimum DSCR by product:**

| Product | Min DSCR |
|---|---|
| DSCR Standard | 1.00 |
| DSCR Expanded — 1.10 tier | 1.10 |
| DSCR Expanded — 1.00 tier | 1.00 |
| DSCR Expanded — 0.80 tier | 0.80 |
| DSCR STR | 1.15 (10% gross haircut) |
| DSCR Multi | 1.00 (15% gross haircut) |
| DSCR Foreign National | 1.00 |
| DSCR No-Ratio | < 1.00 permitted w/ overlays |
| PadSplit | 1.15 |

**Qualifying rent (long-term):** lesser of appraiser Market Rent (1007/1025) or actual lease. Up to 125% of Market Rent allowed if 2-3 months of rent receipts documented. Vacant = Market Rent; DSCR ≥ 1.00 required.

**STR qualifying rent:** actual 12-month average, OR appraiser STR addendum with 3+ STR comps, OR AirDNA Property Earning Potential Report, with 10% haircut.

**Commercial rent (Mixed-Use):** 12+ months remaining lease; 15-25% haircut; ≤ 49.99% of total property income.

|---|---|---|---|
| Purchase / Ltd Cash-Out | 700 | $3M | 80% |
| Purchase / Ltd Cash-Out | 680 | $3M | 75% |
| Cash-Out Refi | 700 | $2.5M | 75% |
| Cash-Out Refi | 680 | $2.5M | 70% |

| Interest-Only | 700 | per tier | -5% from std |

**DSCR ≥ 1.10 (max leverage):**

| Min FICO | Max Loan | Purchase | Ltd Cash-Out | Cash-Out |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 720 | $3M | 80% | 80% | 75% |
| 700 | $2M | 80% | 75% | 75% |
| 680 | $1.5M | 75% | 75% | 70% |

**DSCR 1.00–1.09:** same grid, LTVs shifted down ~5%.

Interest-Only: 75% LTV max. Non-Warrantable Condo: -5% LTV, 1.00+ DSCR, 700+ FICO. Condotel: 70%/65%, 1.00+, 700+.

| Program | Primary | 2nd Home | Investment |
|---|---|---|---|
| Full Doc | 90% | 85% | 80% |
| Bank Statement | 85% | 80% | 80% |
| P&L Only | 80% | 75% | 75% |
| 1099 | 85% | 80% | 80% |
| Asset Utilization | 80% | 75% | 75% |
| Super Jumbo (> $3M) | 75% | 70% | 65% |
| ITIN | 85% | N/A | N/A |
| FN Owner-Occ (2nd home only) | N/A | 65% | N/A |
| Closed-End 2nd Lien | CLTV 85% | CLTV 80% | CLTV 75% |

Max DTI: 43% std, 50% with compensating factors. FICO ≥ 740 + DTI ≤ 43% + 12mo reserves + 0x30/24 req'd for max tiers.

**Non-cumulative adjustments (apply lowest):** declining market -5%, vacant refi -5%, non-warrantable condo -5%, rural (Expanded) -5%, IO -5%, listed for sale in window = based on lesser list/appraised, Foreign National DSCR = 65% max, First-Time Investor = -5% and 1-unit only.

## Transaction types

- **Purchase** (acquired within 180 days)
- **Delayed Purchase** (within 180 days; recoup initial investment only)
- **Rate & Term Refi** (≤ $2k cash; 0x30/12 on prior)
- **Cash-Out Refi** (> $2k cash; caps: Std $500k, Expanded $1M, ≤60% LTV unlimited; FN $500k; No-Ratio $500k)
- **Free & Clear Refi** (≥ 180 days, no prior lien → treated as cash-out, 65% max)
- **Matured Loan Refi** (existing loan past maturity, not KREC)

Cash-Out seasoning: DSCR = none; Non-QM owner-occ = 6-12 mo.

**Non-arm's-length:** LOE required; max 80% LTV on purchase; 12-mo cancelled rent checks for renter-from-landlord.

**IPC:** 6% of PP (DSCR investment); Non-QM owner-occ 6% ≤ 90% LTV / 3% above.

## Prepayment penalty

- DSCR options: 5/3/2/1-year declining, or no-prepay with rate adjustment (+25-75 bps)
- State restrictions: AK/KS/MN/NM/OH no PPP; RI/MI max 1-yr; MO max 2-yr
- PA: ≤ $329,411 (2026) on 1-2 unit no PPP; applies business-purpose regardless of vesting
- NJ: no PPP on individual/LLC/LLP/LP vesting (OK for S/C Corp)
- IL: no PPP on individual vesting
- MS: declining only
- Listed for sale within window: 3-yr PPP required
- DSCR PadSplit: 1-yr PPP min
- DSCR Foreign National: 3-yr PPP min

## Appraisal

- 120 days validity (standard); 121-180 days with 1004D recert; > 180 days = new appraisal
- DSCR Multi: > 6 months = new appraisal (Commercial Narrative required)
- Transfer appraisals NOT accepted on DSCR Multi or Memphis TN
- DSCR Expanded loans > $1.5M: 2 full appraisals
- DSCR Standard: loans > $2M commonly require 2 appraisals
- Non-QM Super Jumbo: 2 full appraisals
- Property listing value floor: lowest of (i) lowest list/sale price in 12 mo Std / 6 mo Expanded+FN, (ii) as-is, (iii) purchase price
- Value seasoning: owned < 180 days with improvements requires 120% cost-basis cap + 1-yr PPP + 1.15 DSCR

## Non-QM ATR — required analysis

Every consumer-purpose loan documents: (1) income/assets, (2) employment status, (3) monthly payment, (4) simultaneous loan payment, (5) taxes/insurance/HOA, (6) other debt obligations, (7) DTI or residual income, (8) credit history.

**HPML** = APR > APOR + threshold. Triggers Reg Z escrow (5 yr min) + §129H appraisal + enhanced ATR. **HOEPA** loans NOT originated except via Closed-End Second Lien Select on select investors.

- Philadelphia: case-by-case on V/F; exception-only on L; case-by-case on C

**Non-QM owner-occupied:** V and F only. DACA = ITIN program on F; not on V. Foreign Nationals = 2nd home only (no primary).

## Investor-specific placement notes

**Investor V:**
- Flagship product — broadest menu
- First-Time Investor DSCR: 700 FICO, 36-mo derogatory seasoning, 1-unit only, must own primary, 12-mo reserves
- No-Ratio: $7.5M/10 agg, $5M/5 cash-out
- DSCR Supreme vs. Investor DSCR = tighter overlays for better pricing
- NYC STR ineligible
- Eff July 2025, wholesale channel halted NY TRID originations; corr + biz-purpose unaffected

**Investor F:**
- Parallel Non-QM suite including ITIN and 2nd Lien Select
- Biz-purpose DSCR brands: Investor DSCR, DSCR Supreme, No-Ratio, Multi
- 2nd Lien Select ineligible MS/NJ/TX
- Exposure: $10M/10 std; $5M/5 cash-out; $7.5M/10 No-Ratio; $5M/5 Multi
- DACA eligible on ITIN with documentation
- FN Owner-Occ 2nd home only (no primary for FNs)

**Investor L:**
- 4 DSCR matrices: Standard, Expanded, Foreign National, Multifamily (5-8 RESIDENTIAL only)
- Bridge Choice = Long-Term Bridge (stabilized pre-DSCR-takeout)
- DSCR Expanded grid: 0.80/1.00/1.10 × 660/680/700/720 FICO
- PadSplit eligible on Expanded (refinance only, 70% LTV, 1.15 DSCR)
- STR: 20% haircut; ineligible in NY
- DSCR Multifamily: NO mixed-use
- FN: entity only, ACH, 3-yr PPP, declining markets ineligible
- Baltimore + Philly ineligible across all Investor L DSCR
- Loans > $1M Committee
- Not in AR/ND/NH/SD/VT; AK/HI exception only
- Does NOT offer Non-QM owner-occupied

**Investor C:**
- Active both Volume I (Fix & Flip / GUC) and Volume II (DSCR)
- DSCR business-purpose only; ineligible ND/NV/SD/UT
- Strong fit for DSCR Standard + low-FICO / mid-loan Expanded
- Does NOT offer DSCR Multi, FN, No-Ratio, or Non-QM owner-occ

**Investor K:**
- Volume I MF Bridge investor only; referenced here only for DSCR takeout scenarios
- NOT a Volume II DSCR Multi placement — use V/F/L instead

## Placement decision rules

- Default: highest leverage at acceptable pricing per loan parameters
- Within 5% of any threshold = pre-screen with ≥ 2 investors
- DSCR loans > $2M = pre-screen with ≥ 2 investors before commit
- DSCR Multi = pre-screen with V or F (or both); L OK for 5-8 Residential-only
- Non-QM owner-occ = pre-screen with both V and F where both eligible; rate-sheet compare
- Committee exceptions = written pre-confirmation from intended investor required
- No investor fit = NOT eligible to originate
