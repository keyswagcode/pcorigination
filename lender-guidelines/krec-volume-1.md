# KREC — Underwriting Guidelines Volume I

Short-Term Investment Lending (Fix & Flip, Ground-Up Construction, Stabilized Bridge, Small Balance Multifamily Bridge)

Effective: April 2026 · Version 1.0

> This is KREC's internal operating/credit policy covering short-term products. Chapter 14 (Investor Salability Matrix) references anonymized investor codes C, T, R, L, K, V, F — these map to KREC's active investor panel (likely BPL, Brokers Advantage, FlipCo, LIC, NQM, ROC, Toorak, TVC, UWM, Verus) and should be cross-referenced with each investor's own guidelines as we ingest them.

## Product summary

| Product | Use | Term | Loan Size | Lien | Recourse |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Fix & Flip | Acquisition + rehab for resale or rent | 6/9/12/18/24 mo | $50k – $5M | 1st | Full |
| Ground-Up Construction | New residential construction on lot | 12/18/24 mo | $200k – $5M | 1st | Full |
| Stabilized Bridge | Short-term stabilized pre-DSCR | 12–24 mo | $100k – $3M | 1st | Full |
| Multifamily Bridge | 5+ unit transitional/value-add | 18–24 mo (+2×6mo ext) | $2M – $5M | 1st | Full |

## Geography

**Ineligible (all products):** ND, SD, VT.
**Fix & Flip / GUC:** NV ineligible; Baltimore City MD ineligible; NYC 5 boroughs additional overlays.
**Multifamily Bridge eligible states only:** NY, NJ, CT, PA, RI, MA, NH, NC, SC, IL, CA (primary & secondary markets only; no tertiary; no rural).
**Market overlays:** Cook County IL (-10% LTAIV/LTARV, 18mo min), Philly zips 19121 & 19132 suspended, Lee/Charlotte/Sarasota/Collier FL (MF Bridge ineligible), Yucca Valley / Joshua Tree / Twentynine Palms CA (-15% LTAIV / -10% LTARV / -10% LTC), Honolulu County HI term minimums, Hawaii Lava Zones 1 & 2 ineligible.
**TX Senate Bill 17:** Non-permanent resident aliens from China/Russia/Iran/NK ineligible on all Volume I.

## Borrower & guarantor

- Entity only (LLC, LP, LLP, C Corp, S Corp, GP for F&F/GUC). No individuals, irrevocable trusts, land trusts, co-ops, fund LLCs, foreign entities, nonprofits, ITIN-only individuals.
- **Multifamily Bridge:** newly formed SPE or clean existing LLC/Corp; max 2 members; all guarantors must be managers.
- Guarantors ≥ 21; ≥ 20% ownership or aggregate to 20%; ≥ 51% of entity ownership must be guarantors on F&F/GUC/Stab Bridge.
- Max 4 guarantors (expandable to 6 with CCO/COO approval).
- US citizens / green card holders eligible; non-permanent resident aliens eligible on F&F/GUC/Stab Bridge with acceptable visa; foreign nationals F&F only (not GUC, not MF Bridge).

## Credit — minimum FICO by product/tier

| Segment | Min Rep FICO |
|---|---|
| F&F Tier 1-2 (0–2 completed) | 680 |
| F&F Tier 3-5 (3+ completed) | 660 |
| GUC Tier 3+ | 680 |
| GUC 100% construction financed | 700 |
| Stabilized Bridge | 680 |
| Multifamily Bridge | 725 |

**Derogatory seasoning:** F&F/GUC/Stab Bridge = 4 yrs post BK/FC/SS/DIL; MF Bridge = 7 yrs.
**Mortgage history:** Max 0x30/12 & 0x60/24 for max leverage; 3x30/36 still eligible; 3x90/36 eligible with -10% LTAIV/LTARV; MF Bridge tolerates zero lates.
**Tax liens:** must be cleared at close; F&F/GUC accepts payment plan ≤ $50k with 3+ months seasoning; MF Bridge = pay in full, no exceptions.
**Ineligible:** first-time homebuyers; first-time investors on MF Bridge.

## Liquidity & reserves

| Product | Liquidity |
|---|---|
| F&F single loan | Greater of 6 mo interest or $25k; > $1M w/ rehab = 10% of construction budget |
| F&F LOC | 20% of line (10% if 10+ completed in 36 mo + 2 active); min $50k |
| GUC | Greater of 10% of construction budget or 6 mo interest |
| Stabilized Bridge | Greater of 6 mo PITIA or $25k, unencumbered |
| MF Bridge | 10% of loan, post-close unencumbered; 3 months of statements, each independently meeting threshold |

**Net worth:** F&F/GUC/Stab Bridge ≥ 10% of loan; MF Bridge ≥ 100% of loan.
**MF Bridge reserves:** 12 months interest + carry (taxes, insurance).

## Property eligibility

**F&F:** NOO 1-unit SFR, townhome, 2-4 unit, PUD, warrantable condo (FL overlays).
**GUC:** 1-unit SFR, 2-4 unit, PUD. No condos (exception: HI CPR and site condos case-by-case).
**Stabilized Bridge:** NOO SFR, 2-4 unit, townhome, PUD. Property occupied or rent-ready.
**MF Bridge:** 100% residential 5+ unit, single property only (no portfolios), no mixed-use, no owner-occupied units, min $100k/unit as-is.

**Universal ineligible:** primary/secondary, co-ops, mobile/manufactured, commercial, cabins/log homes, operating farms, vacation rentals, unique/luxury, dirt roads, agricultural, assisted living, insurance-exempt, underground oil tanks without clean assessment, >10 acre lots, Hawaii Lava Zones 1-2, active solar leases (unless loan reduced by balance), 4+ unit condos >25 years old in FL, deed-restricted.

**Min GLA:** SFR 700 sqft, condo 500 sqft, 2-4 unit 500 sqft/unit.

## Experience tiers (Fix & Flip)

| Tier | Completed (36 mo) | Min FICO | Max ILTC | Max ARLTV |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | 0 | 680 | 80% | 70% |
| 2 | 1-2 | 680 | 80% | 70% |
| 3 | 3-4 | 660 | 90% | 75% |
| 4 | 5-9 | 660 | 90% | 75% |
| 5 | 10+ | 660 | 90% | 75% |

Tier 1: ≤ $1M, purchase/delayed purchase only, no extensive rehab, Level 1-2 submarkets disallowed.
Tier 2-4: ≤ $1M, no extensive rehab, refi ≤ 85% LTV.
Tier 5: all transaction types, cash-out, extensive rehab/additions/ADU permitted.

**GUC:** Tiers 1-2 ineligible. Tier 3 = 2+ flips + 1 GUC / 3yrs; Tier 4 = 3+ flips + 1 GUC / 3yrs; Tier 5 = 3+ flips + GUC Anchor Deal (similar scope, last 48 mo).

## Leverage — key caps

**Fix & Flip:** Up to 90% ILTC / 90% LTFC / 75% ARLTV (Tier 3+, FICO-based).
**GUC:** 75% LTAIV / 75% LTARV / 90% LTC (680 FICO, up to $4M; 720 FICO for $4M-$5M). Cash-out refi capped 65% LTAIV.
**Stabilized Bridge:** Up to 85% ILTC (Tier 3+, 180+ day seasoning) / 70% LTV. DSCR exit test: Tier 2 ≥ 1.10x; Tier 3+ ≥ 1.00x.
**MF Bridge:** 75% LTPP / 70% LTC / 80% TLTC / 75% initial LTV / 70% as-stabilized LTV / 9% NCF debt yield / 1.25x stabilized DSCR.

**Non-cumulative adjustments (apply the lowest resulting leverage):** cash-out -5%, foreign national -5%, NYC 5 boroughs -15%, Suffolk/Nassau -5%, FL/TX -5%, non-warrantable condo (lesser of -10% or 65% cap), rural -15% LTAIV/-10% LTARV, 3x30-3x90 lates -10%.

## Transaction types

- **Purchase** (acquired within 180 days prior to origination)
- **Delayed Purchase** (≤ 180 days since original purchase; recoup initial investment only)
- **Rate & Term Refi** (≤ $2k cash to borrower; existing loan 0x30/12)
- **Cash-Out Refi** (> $2k cash; Tier 3+ only; LTAIV/LTARV capped at 65% on F&F; not permitted on MF Bridge for acquisitions)
- **Free & Clear** (> 180 days seasoning, no prior liens; treated as cash-out)

**Non-arm's-length:** LOE required; renter-from-landlord needs 12 months cancelled checks; employer-to-employee transfers disallowed; max 80% LTV where eligible.
**IPC:** max 6% of purchase price on investor loans.

## Prepayment

- F&F/GUC: no PPP, 3-month minimum interest.
- Stabilized Bridge: investor-dependent (5/4/3/3/3 or 5/4/3/2/1 common).
- MF Bridge: 12-month minimum interest, no PPP.
- **State restrictions:** KS/MI/MN/NM/OH/RI disallow PPP; IL disallows on individual vesting; MS = declining only; NJ disallows for individual/LLC/LLP/LP (allowed for S/C Corp); PA ≤ $329,411 2026 threshold no PPP.

## Insurance

- Hazard deductibles: 5% max (dwelling/fire), 10% (wind/hail/hurricane), NFIP minimums for flood.
- Named-storm/hurricane coverage required for FL/LA.
- 100% coinsurance only; anything lower = ineligible.
- Flood required in FEMA SFHA (A/V zones), amount = lesser of loan or $250k or NFIP max per unit.
- Builder's Risk required on all rehab/construction; max deductible $5k.
- GL ≥ $500k/occurrence & aggregate, no deductible.
- Workers comp per state statute, ≥ $1M/accident.
- Acceptable AM Best B+ / A-VIII or equivalent.

## Investor Salability Matrix — summary (Chapter 14)

Every loan must fit at least one investor's published guidelines. The anonymized investor codes used in the matrix map tentatively to the Drive folders:

- **C** — likely Constructive Capital or Brokers Advantage-style (F&F + GUC focus)
- **T** — likely Toorak (F&F + GUC, wholesale overlays)
- **R** — likely ROC Capital (F&F + Stabilized Bridge + MF Bridge)
- **L** — likely LIC (F&F Cosmetic/Structural + New Construction + Bridge Limited; 4 sub-matrices)
- **K** — likely Kiavi-style (F&F Light/Heavy + Stabilized/Multifamily/Mixed-Use Bridge; tier: New Customer → Ultra-Professional)
- **V** — Verus (Volume II — DSCR)
- **F** — FlipCo or a Non-QM investor (Volume II)

(Mappings will be confirmed as each investor's own guidelines are ingested.)

**Key salability bands (Fix & Flip):**
- < $100k: only Investor R participates
- $1M – $2M: C/T/R/L all participate; $2M+ drops to T and R with Committee
- $3M – $5M: T (Platinum + 720 FICO) and R only
- FICO ≥ 700: all investors at max leverage; 650-679 = Committee/reduced; < 650 ineligible for most

**GUC max loan amounts:** C = $2M · T = $5M · R = $5M · L = $3M · K does NOT purchase GUC.

**Stabilized Bridge:** R (up to $3M, 85% ILTC, 70% LTV) · L Bridge Limited (≥ 700 FICO, 80% LTV) · K (up to $5M+, 90% ILTC, 75% LTV purchase).

**Multifamily Bridge placement:** R (tight state list, 725 FICO, 75% LTPP, 9% DY, 1.25× stabilized DSCR) OR K (broader geography, mixed-use permitted, sponsor tiers, Cook County IL ineligible for MF).

**Placement rules:**
- Loans within 5% of any threshold must be pre-screened with a backup investor.
- F&F > $1M: pre-screen with 2+ investors.
- MF Bridge: always pre-screen with R and/or K before rate lock.
- Any loan that fits no investor = not eligible to originate absent Credit Committee exception with pre-confirmed placement.

## Investor-specific notes

**Investor L:**
- 4 product matrices: Bridge Limited / Rehab Cosmetic / Rehab Structural / New Construction
- Top 100 MSA required for Bridge Limited; Top 300 MSA for Rehab
- Buffalo NY entity address = ineligible
- Detroit, Indianapolis, Cleveland, Baltimore, Philadelphia — exception only
- Ineligible states: AR, ND, NH, SD, VT; AK/HI by exception
- All loans > $1M need Committee approval
- Valuation = 3rd-party external (hybrid) $595 OR full appraisal; no transfer appraisals

**Investor K:**
- 1-4 Family Bridge (Stabilized/Light/Heavy) + Multifamily/Mixed-Use Bridge
- Sponsor tiers: New Customer (0), Novice (1-2), Experienced (3-7), Professional (8-14), Ultra-Pro (15+). Experience aged 3-10 years = 0.5 each.
- Stabilized = rehab ≤ 5% of AIV/PP; Heavy = >50% OR condo conversion OR 20%+ expansion of ≥ 750 sqft OR change of use
- Max 90% LTV/LTC / 75% LTARV residential with excess first-loss position retained by originator
- Baltimore/Philadelphia new borrower needs 3+ prior transactions in that city; HI = 3+ on same island
- Cook County IL: MF ineligible, Residential -10% LTV
- -10% LTV cities: Baltimore MD, Hartford CT, Houston TX
- Max points + broker 5%; Stabilized Bridge max 12 mo term, ≥ 1.10× DSCR

## Ineligible transactions (all products)

- Owner-occupied / consumer-purpose (→ Volume II Non-QM)
- Foreclosure bailouts
- Builder bailouts (builder constructed > 4 independent units in same subdivision/project)
- Land-only
- Mid-construction takeout on GUC (construction started without KREC)
- Proceeds paying personal obligations (child support, alimony, personal tax liens)
- Reno budget > purchase price on MF Bridge
- Heavy reno on MF Bridge
- Condo conversions on MF Bridge (→ F&F)
